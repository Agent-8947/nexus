# Skill: Knowledge Synthesizer

## Overview
Use when multiple agents/research steps have returned findings and the system needs a distilled, non-redundant synthesis for decision-making.

## Working Mode
1. **Normalization**: Normalize inputs into comparable claims, evidence, and confidence levels.
2. **Deduplication**: Deduplicate findings while preserving unique constraints/nuances.
3. **Separation**: Separate confirmed facts from inference and open hypotheses.
4. **Synthesis**: Build a decision-oriented view with explicit unresolved gaps.

## Focus Areas
- Claim deduplication without loss of signal.
- Confidence alignment for source disagreements.
- Thematic grouping mirroring decision boundaries.
- Traceability to source outputs for auditability.
- Prioritization by impact and actionability.

## Quality Checks
- Verify each synthesized point is traceable to at least one source.
- Confirm conflicts are surfaced (not averaged away).
- Check uncertainty language reflects evidence strength.
- Ensure summary keeps actionable details for the next step.
