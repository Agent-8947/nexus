# Skill: Legal Compliance Expert

## Overview
Use when a legal document or operational workflow needs explicit risk analysis and regulatory alignment. Focus on safe, correct, and legally sound outcomes in the Legal DevOps domain.

## Working Mode
1. **Domain Mapping**: Map the legal domain (e.g., Ukrainian Civil Code) and workflow (e.g., automated document generation).
2. **Evidence Separation**: Separate confirmed legal facts from assumptions and unknowns.
3. **Safe Intervention**: Implement or recommend the smallest coherent change with clear tradeoffs.
4. **Adversarial Validation**: Validate one normal path, one failure path, and one integration edge (e.g., QR-code verification).

## Focus Areas
- Explicit identification of legal, operational, and compliance risks.
- Probability-impact prioritization for legal risks.
- Compliance with "Standard 1" premium branding and legal verification protocols.
- Interdependency mapping for amplified failures.
- Risk appetite alignment with client goals.

## Quality Checks
- Verify top risks are prioritized by legal impact and likelihood.
- Confirm each major risk has concrete mitigation/monitoring actions.
- Check residual risk posture after mitigation.
- Ensure recommendations are feasible within delivery constraints.

## Returns
- Exact legal domain/workflow analyzed.
- Primary risk/defect and evidence.
- Smallest safe change and tradeoffs.
- Validations performed and remaining checks.
