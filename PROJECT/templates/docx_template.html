1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The Nexus Framework represents a paradigm shift in autonomous execution, strictly isolating high-performance computational workloads from dynamic business logic to ensure ultimate reliability and scale.

  • Hardware Agnosticism: Operates robustly within rigid 6GB memory constraints.
  • Security-First Paradigm: Leverages Rust core to mathematically eliminate bounds-check vulnerabilities.
  • AI Empowerment: The Jules async integration layer seamlessly bridges complex reasoning with executable logic.
